Yamaha YZF R3 Encyclopedia - Overview

History and Background

The Yamaha YZF R3 was first introduced in 2015 as an entry-level sportbike. It quickly gained popularity among motorcycle enthusiasts due to its sleek design and impressive performance.

Key Features

  • Top Speed: 112 mph
  • Transmission: 6-speed manual
